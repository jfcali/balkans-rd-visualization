WESTERN BALKANS R+1

A brief analysis of the region's Science and Tech panorama

The Western Balkans involves several European countries, each of them with different economics, innovation policies, and distinct scientific/technological production.

The following set of insights are taken from data describing Research & Innovation activities of the region, as well as information about the actors involved in said projects.

A BIRD'S EYE VIEW

The data frames used report projects held across six countries: Serbia, Montenegro, Macedonia, Kosovo, Bosnia, and Herzegovina and Albania, recording projects submitted to different grants and funds provided by the European Union across ten years, from 2007 and 2017.
